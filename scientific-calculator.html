Scientific Calculator

The scientific calculator expands on the functionality of a basic calculator by including additional mathematical functions. In addition to the basic arithmetic operations, a scientific calculator allows you to:

  • Perform trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan) (do not use partheses for value)
  • Calculate exponents (use ** for exponents)
  • Use parentheses for complex calculations
