Basic Calculator

A basic calculator is a simple yet powerful tool that allows you to perform fundamental arithmetic operations. With a basic calculator, you can:

  • Add numbers together
  • Subtract one number from another
  • Multiply numbers
  • Divide one number by another

Basic calculators are great for everyday calculations, such as balancing your checkbook, calculating shopping expenses, or determining simple proportions.
